What is DSA?

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) refer to the study of methods for organizing and storing data and the design of procedures (algorithms) for solving problems, which operate on these data structures.DSA is one of the most important skills that every computer science student must have.

Why DSA is Important?

Data Structures and Algorithms plays a pivotal role in optimization, enabling developers to devise efficient solutions to computational problems. By supporting optimized data structures and algorithms, developers can streamline operations, minimize resource usage, and improve overall system performance.
