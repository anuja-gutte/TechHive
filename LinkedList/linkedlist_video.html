Linked List Explanation:

Video Overview:

This video from Apna College introduces the Linked List data structure, explaining its dynamic nature, and how it overcomes the limitations of arrays. The tutorial covers basic operations such as inserting, deleting, and traversing nodes in a singly linked list.

The lecture also explores different types of linked lists, including singly linked lists, doubly linked lists, and circular linked lists. It concludes with practical examples and coding implementations in C++ and Java, ensuring a thorough understanding of linked list operations and their real-world applications.
